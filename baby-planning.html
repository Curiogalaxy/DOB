Welcome to Your Baby Planning Journey

Preparing for a new baby can feel overwhelming, but with proper planning and organization, you can feel confident and ready for your new arrival. This tool will guide you through the essential steps of baby planning, from early pregnancy to bringing your baby home.

Use this resource to create your personalized baby plan, track your progress, and ensure you have everything you need for this exciting new chapter in your life.

Baby Planning Timeline

First Trimester (Weeks 1-12)

  • Confirm pregnancy with a healthcare provider
  • Start taking prenatal vitamins
  • Research and select an obstetrician or midwife
  • Begin adjusting diet and eliminating unsafe foods

Second Trimester (Weeks 13-27)

  • Announce your pregnancy to family and friends
  • Begin planning the nursery
  • Start researching baby gear essentials
  • Consider childbirth education classes

Third Trimester (Weeks 28-40)

  • Finalize nursery setup
  • Pack your hospital bag
  • Install car seat
  • Finalize birth plan
  • Prepare older siblings (if applicable)

After Birth

  • Schedule pediatrician appointments
  • Understand newborn care basics
  • Plan for postpartum recovery
  • Arrange for help during first weeks
